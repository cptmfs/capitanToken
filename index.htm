About

Dear Earth Citizens,

As you know, sea transportation constitutes 86% of world trade today.

Sea transport is a very cheap mode of transport. According to the data of 2021,sea transportation is 3 times more economical than rail, 7 times more economical than road and 21 times more economical than air transportation.

We think that this cheap and huge amount of money is circulating industry needs crypto technology and we will do our best to solve this.
